01 / PHILOSOPHY

Why
Core
Exists

01
"The child is not a vessel to fill — but a fire to kindle."

Most classrooms measure what children remember. Core measures what they discover. We run a 5-day inquiry-based learning program that replaces passive instruction with genuine exploration, personal research, and collaborative sense-making.

Rooted in the 5E Instructional Model and aligned with NCF 2023 / NEP 2020, Core is a Public Charitable Trust that cross-subsidises access — premium schools fund free seats for government school students.

We are not a school. Not a tuition centre. We are a learning space — and the difference matters.

The 5-Day
Learning Arc

Each week is a complete inquiry cycle. Children move from provocation to publication — as real researchers, creators, and thinkers. Facilitated, never instructed.

1
Monday
PROVOCATION
A hook, a mystery, a contradiction. Children encounter something that demands explanation and choose their own question to pursue.
2
Tuesday
EXPLORATION
Hands-on investigation, field observation, experiments, and research. Facilitated — never instructed.
3
Wednesday
EXPLANATION
Children build meaning from their findings. Connections are made. Vocabulary is owned, not borrowed.
4
Thursday
ELABORATION
Extend thinking. Cross-disciplinary links. New challenges that deepen, not just broaden, understanding.
5
Friday
SHARING
Every child presents. Peer reflection, self-assessment, celebration of the thinking process — not just the product.

02 / Core Dispositions

Six inner
postures we
cultivate

We don't just teach content. We grow the habits of mind that make a lifelong learner.

  • 1Curiosity — asking before assuming. The question matters more than the answer.
  • 2Courage — sharing half-formed ideas. Uncertainty is the beginning of thinking.
  • 3Collaboration — thinking is a social act. Ideas grow in dialogue.
  • 4Reflection — pausing to make meaning. What changed in my thinking?
  • 5Perseverance — sitting with difficulty. Hard problems deserve time.
  • 6Empathy — seeing through other eyes. Other perspectives complete our own.
